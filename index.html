How can I cancel my reservation?

How can I change delivery address after order placement?

Log into your account and go to the "Order History" or "My Orders" section. Locate your order and check if it’s still eligible for address updates. Typically, orders that have already been shipped cannot be modified.

How can I contact my delivery person?

Many delivery services and retailers provide real-time tracking and contact options through their apps or websites. Check the tracking page for your order. Some services offer the ability to message or call the delivery person directly.
